Kelsey Nordstrom, Oboist

      Biography

      Originating from Kenora, Ontario, Kelsey is an oboist currently residing in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Kelsey holds both a Bachelor of Music in Performance and a Post-Baccalaureate Diploma in Performance from the University of Manitoba. She studied with Robin MacMillan of the Winnipeg Symphony Orchestra and has performed in masterclasses for such oboists as Bede Hanley, Sarah Jeffrey, and Charles Hamann. During her studies, she received several awards from the University of Manitoba, including the Marcel A. Desautels Fund Award, the Margaret H. Tyler Award in Music and the Marcel A. Desautels Faculty of Music Scholarship.

      Kelsey is currently a member of the Winnipeg Wind Ensemble. She has performed with local chamber groups, the Royal Canadian Air Force Band, and Cirque Musica. Kelsey was recently awarded an Honorable Mention in the 2020 Sound Espressivo Global Competition. In addition to performing, Kelsey teaches both privately and as a clinician for schools in the Winnipeg area.

      Services
